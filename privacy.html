Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Our Promise

DateScan is 100% on-device AI. Your camera feed, photos, and style analysis never leave your browser. We don't operate an image server — there is literally nowhere for your photos to go.

1. Who We Are

DateScan ("we", "us", "our") is a web-based AI style coach available at https://datescan.app/. For any privacy question, contact [email protected].

2. Age Restriction — 18+ Only

DateScan is intended exclusively for users aged 18 or older. The Service is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect any data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has used DateScan, please contact us immediately and we will take appropriate action.

3. How On-Device AI Works

When you run a scan, Google's MediaPipe face-landmark model is loaded into your browser and executes entirely on your device's own CPU and GPU via WebGL. This means:

  • Your camera feed is processed frame-by-frame on your device.
  • Only textual style suggestions are generated — no images are uploaded.
  • Raw frames, biometric landmarks, and video are discarded the moment you leave the scan screen.
  • We have no "training data" pipeline fed by your photos, because we never receive them.

4. What Data We Do Process

Local storage on your device

DateScan uses your browser's localStorage to remember your progress and preferences between visits. This data never leaves your device and includes:

  • Onboarding completion state
  • Your XP, level, streak, and mission history
  • Your daily free-tier counter (3 scans + 2 AI Coach messages, resets every 24 hours)
  • Whether you have purchased a Pro plan
  • Your cookie-banner consent choice

You can clear all of this at any time by clearing site data for datescan.app in your browser.

Account & subscription data

If you purchase a paid plan, our Merchant of Record Lemon Squeezy collects the data required for billing (name, email, billing address, payment method). DateScan itself never sees or stores your full card number. We receive only a webhook confirming your subscription status.

Infrastructure & server logs

Our static files and service worker are hosted on GitHub Pages, and all traffic is routed through Cloudflare for TLS encryption, DDoS protection, caching, and abuse prevention. Both GitHub and Cloudflare may automatically record standard request metadata — IP address, user agent, timestamps — in their own server logs for security purposes. We do not operate our own analytics database on top of these logs.

5. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not upload or store your photos, video frames, or scan results on any server.
  • We do not use biometric data to identify you.
  • We do not sell data to advertisers or data brokers.
  • We do not require an email or password to use the free tier.

6. Third-Party Services

Your browser loads assets from a small number of vetted providers. None of them ever receive your photos.

  • Lemon Squeezy — payments and Merchant of Record. Privacy policy
  • Cloudflare — CDN, TLS, and security layer. Privacy policy
  • GitHub Pages — static hosting of the app files. Privacy policy
  • Google Fonts — Inter and Playfair Display typefaces. Privacy policy
  • jsDelivr CDN — delivers the MediaPipe face-landmark library. Privacy policy

7. Cookies & Local Storage

DateScan itself does not set any advertising or tracking cookies. We use:

  • LocalStorage — to remember your progress, preferences, and cookie-consent choice.
  • Strictly necessary cookies — set by Cloudflare for security, bot detection, and load balancing.
  • Payment cookies — set by Lemon Squeezy only when you proceed to checkout, for fraud prevention.

On your first visit you will see a cookie banner. By clicking "Accept" you acknowledge the use of the categories above.

8. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or California, you have the following rights regarding any personal data we hold:

  • Right of access — request a copy of your data.
  • Right to erasure — request deletion.
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to object — object to how we process your data.
  • Right to portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to lodge a complaint — with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email [email protected] with the subject "Privacy Request". We respond within 30 days.

9. Data Retention

Because we do not store your images or scan results server-side, there is nothing on our end to retain. LocalStorage data lives on your device until you clear it. Billing records held by Lemon Squeezy are retained as long as required by tax and accounting law.

10. Security

All traffic to datescan.app is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS, terminated at Cloudflare. Because your sensitive data (camera feed) never leaves your device, the primary attack surface is your own device — please keep your browser and operating system up to date.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version. Material changes will be announced on the homepage.
