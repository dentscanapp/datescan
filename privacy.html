Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Plain-English summary

Two things happen in DateScan, and they're treated very differently.

  • Camera scans (outfit, grooming, posture checks) run on your device. Video frames never leave your browser.
  • AI Style Coach chat (text messages and any photos you choose to attach) is sent to our server and forwarded to a third-party vision-language model so you can get a reply. We don't train models on your chats and we don't retain them beyond what's needed to operate the service.

1. Who We Are

DateScan ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered style coach available as a web app at https://datescan.app/ and as an Android app distributed via the Google Play Store. For any privacy question, contact [email protected].

2. Age Restriction — 18+ Only

DateScan is intended exclusively for users aged 18 or older. The Service is not directed at children or teenagers, and we do not knowingly collect any data from anyone under 18. By using DateScan you represent and warrant that you are at least 18 years of age. If we learn that we have collected data from a person under 18, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a minor has used DateScan, please contact us immediately at the address above.

3. How Camera Scans Work (On-Device)

When you run a scan, Google's MediaPipe face-landmark model is loaded into your browser and executes entirely on your device's own CPU and GPU via WebGL/WebAssembly. For the scan feature specifically, this means:

  • Your camera feed is processed frame-by-frame on your device.
  • Raw frames, biometric landmarks, and video are discarded the moment you leave the scan screen.
  • No image data from the scan feature is uploaded to our servers or to any third party.

4. How the AI Style Coach Chat Works (Server-Side)

The AI Style Coach chat is a separate feature from the on-device scan. When you send a message to the Style Coach — with or without an attached photo — the following happens:

  • Your message text, any attached image, and a short recent chat history are sent over HTTPS to our backend at api.datescan.app.
  • Our backend forwards the request to a third-party vision-language model provider (currently OpenAI and/or Anthropic, subject to change) so the model can generate a reply.
  • The reply is streamed back to your device.
  • We retain request logs (message content, image references, timestamp, anonymized user identifier) for up to 30 days for abuse prevention, debugging, and moderation. After 30 days the content is automatically deleted.
  • We do not use your chat content to train AI models. Our third-party providers operate under zero-retention and no-training agreements where available; please consult their policies below for details.
  • If you attach a photo, you are asked for explicit consent via an in-app modal before the upload happens. You can turn off all image uploads in chat by enabling Private Mode in the Privacy panel.

Do not upload photos of other people without their consent, and never upload photos of anyone under 18. You are solely responsible for the content you submit to the Style Coach.

5. Data We Store

On your device (local)

DateScan uses your browser's localStorage and IndexedDB to remember your progress and preferences between visits. This data never leaves your device and includes:

  • Onboarding completion state
  • Your XP, level, streak, and mission history
  • Your digital closet (photos you save yourself)
  • Your point balance (5 points per day, refills every 24 hours) and daily bonus ledger
  • Your cookie-banner and privacy-panel choices

You can erase all of this at any time by opening the Privacy panel in the app and tapping "Erase everything on this device", or by clearing site data for datescan.app in your browser.

On our server

  • AI Style Coach chat logs — retained up to 30 days (see §4).
  • Content moderation reports — when you tap "Report" on an AI response, we store the reported text, your chosen category, and any optional note, retained for up to 12 months for safety auditing.
  • Server access logs — IP address, user agent, request path, timestamp, retained for up to 14 days for security, retained longer in aggregated/anonymized form for analytics.

Advertising data

DateScan shows optional rewarded video ads via Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). You can earn +2 points by voluntarily watching one. AdMob may collect an advertising identifier and standard device/network data to serve and measure those ads. We never initiate an ad on your behalf — it only runs when you tap "Watch a short video" in the Earn Points panel. See Google Ads privacy.

Infrastructure & server logs

Our static files are served via Cloudflare, which handles TLS termination, DDoS protection, caching, and abuse prevention. Cloudflare automatically records standard request metadata (IP address, user agent, timestamps) in its own server logs for security purposes.

6. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not upload camera-scan frames, scan results, or on-device biometric landmarks to any server.
  • We do not use facial recognition or any other biometric data to identify you.
  • We do not sell data to advertisers or data brokers.
  • We do not serve behavioural advertising inside the app.
  • We do not use your Style Coach messages or photos to train AI models.
  • We do not require an email or password to use the free tier on the web.

7. Third-Party Services

The following third-party providers are involved in running DateScan:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) — serves the optional rewarded videos users can watch to earn bonus points. Privacy policy
  • OpenAI and/or Anthropic — vision-language model providers for the AI Style Coach chat. Chat messages and attached images are forwarded to them to generate replies. We use API terms that prohibit training on user data where available. OpenAI privacy · Anthropic privacy
  • Cloudflare — CDN, TLS, DDoS protection, and security layer. Privacy policy
  • Google Fonts — Inter and Playfair Display typefaces. Privacy policy
  • jsDelivr CDN — delivers the MediaPipe face-landmark library. Privacy policy

8. Cookies & Local Storage

DateScan itself does not set any advertising or tracking cookies of its own. We use:

  • LocalStorage & IndexedDB — to remember your progress, point balance, preferences, closet, and cookie-consent choice on your device.
  • Strictly necessary cookies — set by Cloudflare for security, bot detection, and load balancing.
  • Ad-network cookies / identifiers — set by Google Mobile Ads only when you voluntarily watch a rewarded video to earn bonus points.

On your first visit you will see a cookie banner with both Accept and Reject options. Rejecting only disables non-essential storage; strictly necessary cookies (security) remain active because the service cannot function without them.

10. Data Deletion

You have three ways to delete your data:

  • In-app wipe (local data only): Open the Privacy panel inside DateScan and tap "Erase everything on this device". This wipes your closet, scan history, local settings, and cached service worker data from the current device.
  • Web-based account deletion (local + server data): Visit https://datescan.app/delete-account.html and submit the form. No login is required. We will permanently delete your chat logs, moderation reports, and entitlement records associated with your email/device identifier within 30 days. This URL is also the account deletion link we submit to the Google Play Store, in compliance with Google Play's account deletion policy.
  • Email request: Write to [email protected] with the subject "Data Deletion Request".

DateScan is free and no subscriptions exist, so there is nothing to cancel — deleting your data simply wipes it from your device and our servers.

11. Data Retention

We retain different categories of data for different periods:

  • On-device data — kept on your device until you delete it.
  • AI Style Coach chat logs — up to 30 days for abuse prevention and debugging, then automatically deleted.
  • Content moderation reports — up to 12 months for safety auditing.
  • Server access logs — up to 14 days in raw form.

12. International Data Transfers

Our backend and AI model providers may process your data in the United States and other countries outside your country of residence. Where required, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and the EU-US Data Privacy Framework to transfer data lawfully out of the EEA/UK.

13. Security

All traffic to datescan.app and api.datescan.app is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. We follow industry-standard security practices including encryption at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews. No system is 100% secure, however — please keep your device, browser, and Google account up to date and notify us immediately if you suspect a security issue.

14. AI-Generated Content & Content Moderation

The AI Style Coach produces machine-generated responses and can make mistakes, including giving inaccurate or inappropriate advice. You can report any AI response by tapping the "Report" button below the message. We review every flagged response and use the feedback to improve the service and enforce our content policies. Certain categories of input are blocked client-side and server-side, including: content involving minors, sexually explicit content, and content encouraging self-harm. See our Terms of Service for the full list.
