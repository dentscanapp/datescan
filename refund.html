Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Is DateScan really free?

Yes. There are no subscriptions, no paid tiers, no trial-to-paid upgrades, and no in-app purchases. The service is funded in part by optional rewarded video ads that only play when a user explicitly taps "Watch a short video" in the Earn Points panel.

Do I need an account or a credit card?

No. DateScan works without any sign-up. Your point balance and daily bonus ledger are stored locally in your browser's storage.

What happens when I run out of points?

A 24-hour countdown starts. After it elapses, your balance is automatically refilled to 5 points. You can also earn bonus points at any time by rating a scan, sharing an outfit, or watching a rewarded video — subject to the per-day and per-hour caps above.

Are refunds possible?

There is nothing to refund because DateScan is free. If you previously had a paid subscription that is still being billed, contact support and we'll cancel it and issue any refund to which you're entitled.

How do the rewarded ads work?

When you tap "Watch a short video" in the Earn Points panel, Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) serves a rewarded video. You earn +2 points once the ad finishes playing. Ads are capped at one per hour. We never serve interstitial, banner or pop-up ads anywhere in the product.

Can I still earn points if I clear my browser data?

Yes, but your existing balance and bonus-claim history will be reset — so you may lose any bonus points you had already banked today. The daily 5-point allowance itself will be available again immediately.
