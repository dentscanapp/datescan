What to Include

To help us help you faster, please include:

  • What you were trying to do — "I clicked Scan Outfit and..."
  • What actually happened — error message, frozen screen, wrong result, etc.
  • Your device and browser — e.g. "iPhone 15, Safari" or "Windows 11, Chrome 120"
  • A screenshot if the issue is visual
  • Your purchase email if it's a billing question

Common Issues

"My camera won't turn on"

Check that you've granted camera permission to datescan.app in your browser settings. On iOS, you may also need to go to Settings → Safari → Camera and set it to "Ask" or "Allow". Make sure no other app is currently using your camera. Try reloading the page.

"The scan gave me a weird result"

Lighting matters a lot. Face a window or a bright lamp — avoid backlighting yourself with a window behind you. Keep your face centered and about arm's length from the camera. If you're wearing a dark outfit against a dark background, the outfit color detection may struggle.

"I lost my Pro access after clearing cookies"

Pro access on DateScan is tied to your browser's local storage. If you cleared site data, restore your purchase from the in-app Settings → "Restore purchases" button, or reach out via social media DM with your Google Play order ID.

"I want a refund"

See our Refund Policy for details. Refunds on Google Play subscriptions are handled directly through your Play Store account within 48 hours of purchase.

"I have feedback or a feature idea"

We love feedback. Seriously. DM us on any of our social channels (links in the footer). If your idea makes it into the product, we'll let you know.

"I'm a journalist / creator / partner"

DM us on X or LinkedIn with "Press" or "Partnership" in your message and we'll route it to the right person.

Privacy Requests & Data Deletion

To delete your data, you have two options:

  • Fastest — web form (no login required): visit datescan.app/delete-account.html and submit the form. We'll permanently delete your chat logs, moderation reports, and subscription records within 30 days.
  • In-app wipe (local data only): Open the Privacy panel inside the app and tap "Erase everything on this device".

For other GDPR/UK GDPR/CCPA requests (access, rectification, portability, objection), use the same deletion form and specify your request in the "Reason" field. See our Privacy Policy for the full list of rights and how we handle them.

Reporting Harmful AI Content

If the AI Style Coach gave you a response that is offensive, dangerous, sexual, or otherwise inappropriate, the fastest way to report it is the "Report" button directly under the message in the chat. This sends us the exact response, context, and metadata automatically.

If you believe any content on DateScan may involve a minor or constitute illegal activity, please use the Report button with the category marked "URGENT — Safety" so we can escalate it to the top of the queue.

Response Times

  • Technical issues — usually within 1–2 business days
  • Billing and refunds — within 3 business days
  • Feature requests — we read all of them but may not reply individually
  • Press inquiries — within 1 business day

We don't have a phone number or a live chat — yet. The channels above are the fastest way to reach us, and the quality of support is better when we can look at your screenshots and logs through the in-app Report button before responding.
