Before we turn on your camera

The live scanner runs entirely on your device. Here's what that means.

  • Live scanner = on-device. When you use the camera scanner, frames are analysed inside your browser by an on-device AI model. They are never uploaded to our servers and we never see them.
  • Nothing is saved. Frames live in your device's memory for a few seconds while the AI checks outfit, grooming, posture and lighting — then they're discarded.
  • You're in control. Stop the camera any time by closing the scanner. Your browser also lets you revoke camera permission at any moment.
  • Self-scan only. Point the camera at yourself, not other people. Scanning anyone else without their consent is forbidden by our Terms.
  • Note: The AI Style Coach chat is different — if you upload a photo there, that image is sent to our vision model. You'll see a separate notice the first time.

See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for full details.

Before you upload an image

Two things to know — what we do with your image, and what you must not upload.

  • Your image is sent to our AI vision model via api.datescan.app so the Style Coach can "see" it and reply. It's processed for that single message and not retained on our server beyond the response.
  • No training. Chat images are not used to train any AI model.
  • Only upload pictures of yourself. DateScan is a self-coaching tool, not a way to analyse other people.
  • No other people. No friends, ex-partners, colleagues, strangers, celebrities or public figures — even if the photo is publicly available online.
  • Absolutely no minors. Zero tolerance. Any image containing a person under 18 — including yourself as a child — is forbidden and will result in immediate termination.
  • No nudity, sexual or harmful content. See our Acceptable Use policy for the full list.

Violations may be reported to authorities. See Terms of Service, sections 5.1–5.3.

Privacy & Data

What leaves your device

  • ●
    Camera scan & analysis. Runs 100% on your device using MediaPipe + WebAssembly. Your video frames never leave the browser.
  • ●
    Closet & history. Stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB. Nothing is sent anywhere.
  • ●
    AI Style Coach Chat — text. Your messages are sent to api.datescan.app and forwarded to a vision-language model so you can get a reply.
  • ●
    AI Style Coach Chat — images. If you attach a photo or pick one from your closet, that image is uploaded with your message for the model to "see". It is processed for that single request and not retained on our server beyond the response. We do not use chat content to train models.
