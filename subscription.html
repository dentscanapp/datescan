How to Cancel

All Weekly, Monthly, and Yearly plans are auto-renewing subscriptions managed by Google Play. You can cancel at any time — cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period, and you keep Pro access until then. To avoid being billed for the next period, cancel at least 24 hours before the renewal date.

To cancel on your Android phone:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app.
  2. Tap your profile icon (top right) → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.
  3. Tap DateScan.
  4. Tap Cancel subscription and follow the prompts.

You can also cancel from any browser by opening play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions and signing in with the Google account you used at checkout.

If you can't find the subscription in your Google Play account, you may have purchased with a different Google account — try signing in with each Google account you use on your device. If you still can't locate it, email info@datescan.app and we'll help you track it down.

The Single Scan product is a one-time in-app purchase — there is nothing to cancel.

How to Restore Pro on a New Device

Your Pro entitlement is tied to the Google account you purchased with. To restore Pro on a new Android device:

  1. Install DateScan from the Google Play Store on the new device.
  2. Make sure you're signed in with the same Google account you used to purchase Pro.
  3. Open the app. Pro should be recognized automatically the first time the app connects to Google Play.
  4. If Pro doesn't activate, open the app menu and tap Restore purchases.

If Pro still doesn't activate after a restore attempt, email info@datescan.app with the subject "Restore Pro" and include your Google Play order ID (it starts with GPA. and can be found in your Google Play order history). We'll verify with Google and help you get your entitlement back, typically within 1–2 business days.

How to Change Your Plan

To upgrade or downgrade (e.g. from Weekly to Monthly, or Monthly to Yearly):

  1. Open Google Play Store → profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.
  2. Tap DateScan.
  3. Select a new plan — Google Play handles the upgrade or downgrade, including any pro-rated credit for your current plan, automatically.

Plan changes take effect immediately for upgrades, and at the end of the current billing period for downgrades, in line with Google Play's standard subscription rules.

Billing, Receipts, and Invoices

Google Play sends a receipt to your Google account email address automatically after each successful payment. To view, download, or re-send a receipt:

  1. Go to play.google.com/store/account/orderhistory.
  2. Sign in with the Google account you purchased with.
  3. Find the DateScan order and tap it to view or re-send the receipt.

Google Play collects and remits all applicable VAT / sales tax based on your billing location — we do not handle any of that ourselves. If you need a VAT-compliant invoice for business expenses, Google Play generates one automatically; you can download it from your order history.

Refunds

Refunds for Google Play purchases are handled by Google according to the Google Play refund policy. Google typically allows self-service refunds within 48 hours of purchase. See our Refund Policy for the full story, including the discretionary 14-day goodwill window we offer beyond Google's self-serve period.
